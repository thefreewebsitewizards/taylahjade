About Taylah Jade

A year ago, I dropped out of my engineering degree, gave up my scholarship, and went all in on myself—with zero guarantees and a $40K investment in the first 3 months. Today, I run a soul-led business that fuels my freedom. I've worked from Thailand to Europe, had $7K sales weeks, built a dream team, and coached high-achievers to make success non-negotiable—all while living a life that feels as good as it looks.

I'm a personal development coach helping ambitious people build the mindset to unlock next-level business, balance, and bold goals—with pleasure, purpose, and resilience at the core. This isn't just business. It's personal.
